v58 M10 validation

Camera / facing validation:
1. Enter a generated level.
2. Confirm the player avatar faces away from the camera in the expected third-person direction.
3. Confirm the front cone points the same way the player is facing.
4. Hold right click and move the mouse.
5. Confirm the camera rotates around the player while keeping the avatar-facing relationship sensible.

Targeting validation:
1. Walk near a cluster of incomplete tiles.
2. Face diagonally across them.
3. Confirm the highlight prefers a tile about 1.5-2 tiles ahead rather than a very near diagonal tile.
4. Press F and confirm the expected highlighted tile is the one completed.
