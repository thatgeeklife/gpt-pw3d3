v58 M13 validation

Color codes:
1. Enter a generated level.
2. Verify incomplete tiles show alphanumeric color codes above them.
3. Verify completed tiles hide the code and show the orb.

Color selection:
1. Press Q and E.
2. Verify the top-left HUD cycles through available colors.
3. Verify the swatch, code, and index update correctly.

Color-match completion:
1. Highlight a tile.
2. Throw the wrong selected color with F.
3. Verify the projectile still uses the selected color from the moment it was thrown.
4. Verify the tile does not complete.
5. Select the correct color and throw again.
6. Verify the tile completes and shows its permanent orb.

Progress HUD:
1. Complete several tiles.
2. Verify the top-right HUD updates:
	- level name
	- whole percent complete
	- completed / total
3. Verify percent is floored and does not show 100% before the level is fully finished.

Minimap:
1. Verify the minimap appears in the lower left while in a generated level.
2. Verify incomplete tiles are shown in near-black.
3. Verify completed tiles show their true color.
4. Verify the player marker and facing arrow move/rotate correctly.
